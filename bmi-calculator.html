Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is BMI?
BMI stands for Body Mass Index, a number based on height and weight.

2. What BMI is considered normal?
A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy.

3. Is this BMI tool accurate?
Yes, it uses the official WHO BMI formula.
