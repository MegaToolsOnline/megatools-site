About This Tool

The Loan EMI Calculator helps you calculate your monthly installment for any loan. Enter loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to get accurate EMI, total interest, and total payment instantly. Perfect for personal, car, home, or business loans.

How to Use

  1. Enter the total loan amount.
  2. Enter the annual interest rate (in percentage).
  3. Enter the loan tenure in months.
  4. Click "Calculate EMI" to see your monthly installment.
