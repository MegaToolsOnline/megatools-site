About This Tool

The Timer tool allows you to set a countdown timer for any activity like workouts, study, or cooking. Start, pause, and reset easily with a modern interface. Free and online tool accessible on any device.

How to Use

  1. Enter the time in minutes.
  2. Click "Start" to begin the countdown.
  3. Click "Pause" to pause the timer.
  4. Click "Reset" to reset the timer back to 00:00.
