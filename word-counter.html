About This Word Counter

This Word Counter tool counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs. It also estimates reading time and provides fast quick-actions (uppercase, lowercase, title case). The tool runs fully in your browser for privacy and speed — no server calls required.

How to Use

  1. Paste or type your text into the box above.
  2. Click Analyze or press Ctrl+Enter / Cmd+Enter to get live counts.
  3. Use Copy, Clear or Download to manage your text. Use quick actions to change case.

FAQs

Q: Is this tool free?
A: Yes — free and works offline in your browser.

Q: How is reading time calculated?
A: Reading time is estimated assuming ~200 words per minute. You can adjust mentally for slower/faster readers.
